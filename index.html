A Few Words About Us

Pan & Coffee is a bakery and coffee shop established in 2020 which specializes in contemporary Mexican breads, gourmet organic coffee, and home-style cooking.

Our menu in inspired by the fusion of American and Mexican cuisine, taking the best from both worlds to create a unique, contemporary dining experience.

At Pan&Coffee we are proud of continuously offering fresh and high-quality ingredients. We want to share our passion for novel tastes and exquisite food and drinks. We know that the right recipe in the correct place will always be appreciated.

Baked with love, baked from home.
